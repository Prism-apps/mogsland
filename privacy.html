Privacy Policy

Prism ("we") sets forth this Privacy Policy regarding the handling of users' information in our app "Mogs Land: Learning Games" (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information through analytics and crash-reporting tools in order to maintain and improve the service:

  • Device ID (an identifier used to distinguish devices)
  • Usage Data (in-app actions such as screens viewed and features used)
  • Crash Data (diagnostic logs collected when the App crashes or encounters an error)

The App does not directly collect any personal information, such as a user's name or email address, that could identify an individual. In-app purchases are processed through Apple Inc.'s system.

2. Third-Party Services We Use

The App uses the following third-party services to collect the information described above:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) — collects Usage Data to help us understand how the App is used
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) — collects Crash Data and Device ID to help us identify and fix technical issues

These services may handle information in accordance with their own privacy policies. For details, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Purpose of Use

The information described above is used solely to operate, maintain, and improve the App, including identifying and fixing bugs and understanding how features are used. It is not used for behavioral advertising, and it is not combined with information that identifies an individual user.

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

Other than sharing information with the third-party services listed in Section 2, we will not disclose any information we obtain to third parties, except as required by law.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for children and is listed in Apple's Kids Category. The App does not display third-party advertising and does not collect any information that could be used to contact or identify a child outside the App. The Device ID, Usage Data, and Crash Data described above are used only for the internal purposes described in this policy and are not used to track children across apps or websites operated by other companies.

6. External Services

In-app purchases use the mechanism provided by Apple.
Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.
